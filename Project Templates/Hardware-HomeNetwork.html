Home Network

This is an ongoing smaller project of mine. Since I am currently renting there isnt anything permanent or really exciting done. At home I currently have A Ubiquiti Dream Machine Pro Managing the network and acting as a hub for my personal machine, home server, and partner's machine. Wifi is taken care of by a Ubiquiti U6 Pro and a bridged Netgear Nighthawk if I need a faster connection to my server than a device's wifi can provide. Any smart devices that I own are controlled and managed through Google Home.
